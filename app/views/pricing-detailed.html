What you pay for

Start with a free trial account

You can sign up and test your web app with a free trial account for up to three months, with no obligations to pay anything while you decide whether GOV.UK PaaS is for you. You’ll get to use trial-sized editions of most of our backing services too.

Scale up to a full account with no long term contract

During the trial period, you can move up to a full paid account any time, and get to use all of the functionality of the PaaS.

You don’t have to commit to a long term contract, and you can enable and disable apps, databases and services as required.

We will charge you based on your usage, and in particular:

  • The amount of memory your app instances use and for how long
  • Which database you use and how many resources it uses:
    • For PostgreSQL or MySQL, price is based on how powerful the server is
    • For MongoDB, ElasticSearch and Redis price is based on storage used
  • What other services you use

Our pricing includes the costs of running the platform, such as patching operating systems and monitoring the infrastructure.

Get the support that suits you and your budget

We offer different support tiers. They scale according to the platform usage of your service, but have a different minimum fee.

Contact gov-uk-paas-support@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk if you want to discuss bespoke support for your service.
